# ChainRepute - Cross-Chain Identity & Reputation Protocol

ChainRepute is a cross-chain identity and reputation protocol that unifies your Stellar and Polkadot reputation, creating verifiable credentials for DeFi, DAOs, and communities.

## Overview

Your reputation follows you across chains. ChainRepute uses AI to scan your activity on both Stellar and Polkadot, generating unified reputation scores and issuing verifiable cross-chain credentials.

### Features

- Cross-chain reputation scanning across Stellar and Polkadot
- AI-powered analysis of on-chain behavior
- Verifiable credentials stored on both chains
- Integration with DeFi protocols and DAOs
- Community access verification
